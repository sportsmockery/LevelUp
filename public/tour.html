Upload a Match Video
Record or pick a match video and LevelUp's AI analyzes every frame.
  1. Pick or record your match video from your phone's library or camera.
  2. Choose your match style — Youth Folk, HS Folk, College, Freestyle, or Greco.
  3. Identify your wrestler — LevelUp detects both wrestlers and you tap "This Is Me."
  4. Add context (optional) — weight class, tournament name, round number.
  5. Hit Analyze — LevelUp extracts key frames, detects action, and scores your technique.
Smart Frame Detection
LevelUp scans your entire video at 1fps, detects action windows using motion analysis, and selects the most important frames — so nothing gets missed.
